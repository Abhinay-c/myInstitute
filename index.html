NITW

National Institute of Technology Warangal (NIT Warangal or NITW) is a public technical and research university located in Warangal, India. It is recognised as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India.

Time Square

Times Square- The most popular hangout place for almost all students undoubtedly , this is the place where most of the memories are made, be it Ethnic night or DJ nights or just relax with friends after a long day.

Library

Library - One of the best libraries in the country, with almost 1 lakh books.

Electrical Department

The Department of Electrical Engineering, established in 1959, is one of the oldest departments of the National Institute of Technology, Warangal.
